Buttermilk fried chicken

Description

Buttermilk fried chicken that's incredibly tender, thanks to tangy buttermilk. After the buttermilk soak, dredge the chicken pieces in seasoned flour and fry them in hot oil until crisp and golden.

    For the chicken

  • Whole chicken (cut into eight pieces)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • White pepper
  • Dried rosemary
  • Ground thyme
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried sage
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Buttermilk

    • For the seasoned flour

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • White pepper
  • Onion powder

    • Peanut oil (for frying)

  1. Step 1. Toss the chicken pieces in seasonings, then marinate in buttermilk overnight.
  2. Step 2. Make the seasoned flour.
  3. Step 3. Dredge each buttermilk-soaked chicken piece in flour and transfer to a plate.
  4. Step 4. Fry the chicken until an instant read thermometer reads 165 degrees F.
  5. Step 5. Drain on a cooling rack over a paper towel-lined baking sheet.
